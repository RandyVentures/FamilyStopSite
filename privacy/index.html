Who we are

FamilyStop is operated by Randy Ventures. Questions about this policy can be sent to randyventures06@gmail.com.

Information we handle

FamilyStop handles the following categories of information depending on how you use the app:

  • Precise location. When you use Explore, Emergency Mode, or road trip features, the app sends your device coordinates to the FamilyStop API to find and rank nearby stops. Location is linked to your active session token and is used only to fulfill the request — it is not stored as a history log.
  • Account and session identifiers. When you first open the app, a guest session token is assigned. If you sign in with your email, an authenticated session token is issued. These identifiers are used to associate saved places, trips, reviews, verifications, and your profile with your account.
  • User content. Reviews you write, verifications you submit for place amenities, places you save, and road trip itineraries you create are stored server-side and linked to your account. Submitted reviews and verifications may be visible to other users as part of the FamilyScore system.
  • Purchases. If you subscribe to FamilyStop Plus, purchase information is processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. We receive entitlement status (whether you hold a Plus subscription) to unlock premium features. Payment details are handled entirely by Apple and are never seen by us.
  • Diagnostics. The app and API may produce diagnostic information such as error logs, crash reports, and performance signals when services are initialized or errors occur. This information is used to maintain app quality and fix bugs.
  • Support communications. When you contact support by email, we handle the information you include — such as your email address, message, device model, iOS version, or screenshots — to respond to and resolve your request.

How we use information

  • To find and rank family-friendly stops near your location.
  • To provide Emergency Mode with the nearest available stops.
  • To store your saved places, trips, reviews, and verifications.
  • To calculate and update FamilyScore ratings from community verifications.
  • To verify your FamilyStop Plus entitlement and unlock premium features.
  • To send sign-in verification codes to your email address when you choose email authentication.
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • To maintain app functionality, detect errors, and comply with App Store requirements.

Third-party services

FamilyStop uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Mapbox. Used for map tiles, geocoding, and route calculation for road trip planning. Mapbox receives location and search queries when you use map features.
  • RevenueCat. Used to manage FamilyStop Plus subscription entitlements. RevenueCat verifies purchase status and sends webhooks to the API when subscriptions renew, expire, or are cancelled.
  • Apple. Processes App Store payments and in-app purchase billing. The iOS platform also handles location permissions, Screen Time integration where applicable, and app distribution.
  • Resend. Used to send transactional sign-in verification codes to your email address when you choose email authentication. Resend handles outbound email delivery.

We do not control third-party services and cannot guarantee their availability. Each third party operates under its own privacy policy.

Storage and sharing

Your account data, saved places, trips, reviews, and verifications are stored server-side to enable the app to work across devices and sessions. Precise location is transmitted for each request but is not stored as a location history.

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with Apple, Mapbox, RevenueCat, and Resend only as necessary for app functionality as described in this policy.

Community-submitted reviews and verifications may be visible to other FamilyStop users as part of the FamilyScore system.

Your choices

  • You can control location access through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.
  • You can use the app as a guest without providing an email address. Some features such as saved places and trips require a session token.
  • You can manage or cancel your FamilyStop Plus subscription through your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • You can restore purchases from inside the app.
  • You can contact support to ask privacy questions, request data deletion, or get help with your account.
  • Deleting the app removes locally cached data from your device, subject to iOS backup behavior. Server-side account data is retained until you request deletion.

Children

FamilyStop is designed to be used by parents and caregivers. The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version here and revise the date at the top of this page.
